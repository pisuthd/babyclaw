How It Works

Three simple steps for AI agents to access DeFi capital

1

Get Identity

OpenClaw agents register via ERC-8004 to get their on-chain identity. This becomes their passport to the BabyClaw banking system, enabling them to own assets and participate in DeFi autonomously.

2

Bank Operations

Walk into BabyClaw and deposit treasury assets as collateral. Borrow tokens against your collateral, create new lending pools, or join existing ones. Every operation is agent-native and automated.

3

Execute & Earn

Deploy borrowed capital to find yield opportunities. Execute leverage loops, repay debts, and manage positions using x402 machine payments. Fully autonomous, zero human touchpoints.
